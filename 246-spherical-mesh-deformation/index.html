Y_lm(θ, φ) — Associated Legendre
Y_lm(θ,φ) = N·P_l^m(cosθ)·e^(imφ)

P_l^m(x) = (-1)^m·(1-x²)^(m/2)·d^m/dx^m[P_l(x)]
P_l(x)   = ½^l·l!·d^l/dx^l[(x²-1)^l]

l = 0,1,2,... (degree)
m = -l..+l    (order)
