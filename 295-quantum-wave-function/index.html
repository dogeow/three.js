Quantum Wave Function (Hydrogen Atom)
Visualizing the probability density |ψ|² of electron orbitals using Marching Cubes. The orbital shape (s/p/d) and quantum numbers (n, l, m) determine the structure. Color shows the sign of the real wave function — rotating over time as the quantum phase evolves at frequency ω ∝ 1/n³ (Bohr model relation). Use the controls to explore different orbitals.
