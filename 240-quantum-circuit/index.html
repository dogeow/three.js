⚞ Quantum Circuit Basics Qubits are horizontal wires. Gates are 3D shapes that transform qubit states.

H → Hadamard (creates superposition)
X → Pauli-X (bit flip)
Y → Pauli-Y
Z → Pauli-Z (phase flip)
CNOT → Controlled-NOT (entangles control+target)
Use lil-gui (top-right) to switch circuits. Drag to orbit.
